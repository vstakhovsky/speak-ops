interviews.---### Why SpeakOps ExistsIT, Product, and AI professionals often have **passive** English vocabulary from reading technical blogs, documentation, and conference talksвҖ”but struggle to use it actively in spoken contexts like:- рҹ”ё **Senior PM interviews** вҖ” "Tell me about a time you handled stakeholder conflict"- рҹ”ё **Stakeholder meetings** вҖ” "We need to discuss roadmap Q2 achievements"- рҹ”ё **Conflict and pushback** вҖ” "I have concerns about this timeline"**SpeakOps bridges this gap** through systematic voice practice with:- рҹҺҜ **Spoken naturalness filtering** вҖ” Rejects written/AI-like phrases, prioritizes natural spoken Business English- рҹӨ– **Claude Code skills** вҖ” Automated phrase extraction, drill building, and scoring- рҹ‘Ҙ **Agent-based development** вҖ” Specialized agents for quality, security, and review- вң… **Eval-driven quality** вҖ” Every behavior has eval coverage with quality gates- рҹ”’ **Privacy-first architecture** вҖ”
